About us:
DBTK has never stopped moving forward and has continuously evolved by further cultivating their relationship with every individual alike and the community as a whole Don’t Blame the Kids Apparel Co. or DBTK is the by-product of a dream, vision, and multiple businesses that was once handled by brothers Emil and Vince Javier. With remarkable messages and unique artistry in each piece they produce, both of them were able to reach out and highlight the youth in every person they came across and positively influence those who believe in what they do.
